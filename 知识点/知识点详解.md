## 二、句型结构 (Sentence Patterns)

### 1. 基本句型：SV, SVC, SVO

#### SV 句型 (主谓结构)
**公式**: S (subject) + V (verb)  
**含义**: 主语做什么，什么动作发生在主语身上

**例句**:
- The bus **stops**. (公交车停了)
- The school gate **opens**. (校门开了)
- Yiming **smiles**. (一鸣微笑)
- It **rains**. (下雨了)

#### SVC 句型 (主系表结构)
**公式**: S (subject) + V (linking verb) + C (complement)  
**含义**: 主语是什么，怎么样

**例句**:
- My favourite colour **is** green.
- The dress **looks** nice!
- We **are** middle school students.
- Harry **is** my friend.
- It **'s** cold.
- The water **feels** warm.
- I **am** sorry.

#### SVO 句型 (主谓宾结构)
**公式**: S (subject) + V (verb) + O (object)  
**含义**: 主语对宾语做了什么动作

**例句**:
- Yiming **opens** the schoolbag.
- Bailing **asks** a question.
- The students **are introducing** themselves.
- They **wear** red scarves.

## 三、一般现在时 (Present Simple)

### 1. 基本用法
用于表达：
1. 日常习惯、例行事务、预定事件
2. 一般事实和真理

**例句**:
- We walk to school every day.
- The school bus arrives at 8 o'clock.
- The sun rises in the east and sets in the west.
- The school year starts in September and ends in June.

### 2. 句型变化

| 人称 | 肯定句 | 否定句 |
|------|-------|-------|
| I/You/We/They | like the school garden | don't (do not) like loud music |
| He/She/It | likes the school garden | doesn't (does not) like loud music |

| 疑问句类型 | 结构 | 回答 |
|----------|------|------|
| Yes/No问句 (Do) | Do I/you/we/they run fast? | Yes, I/you/we/they do. / No, I/you/we/they don't. |
| Yes/No问句 (Does) | Does he/she/it run fast? | Yes, he/she/it does. / No, he/she/it doesn't. |
| Wh-问句 | What time does your school start? | |
| Wh-问句 | How do you go to school? | |
| Wh-问句 | Where do they have class? | |

### 3. 动词第三人称单数变化规则

| 动词类型 | 变化规则 | 示例 |
|---------|---------|------|
| 大多数动词 | +s | he likes |
| 以s, x, sh, ch结尾的动词 | +es | she misses, she washes, it watches |
| 以"辅音字母+y"结尾的动词 | 去y变i，再+es | he cries, he goes |
| 以o结尾的动词 | +es | he goes |
| 不规则动词 | 特殊变化 | she has |

## 四、现在进行时 (Present Continuous)

### 1. 基本结构

| 主语 | Be动词 | 主要动词 | 其他 |
|------|--------|---------|------|
| I | am/am not | playing | in the yard |
| You/We/They | are/are not/aren't | playing | in the yard |
| He/She/It | is/is not/isn't | playing | in the yard |

### 2. 用法
用于描述：
1. 说话时正在进行的动作
   - e.g. Look! My brothers **are playing** football.
2. 当前一段时间内断断续续进行的动作
   - e.g. My sister **is studying** for her exams these days.
   - e.g. My brother **is doing** an experiment these days.

### 3. 动词-ing形式变化规则

| 动词类型 | 变化规则 | 示例 |
|---------|---------|------|
| 大多数动词 | +ing | eat → eating |
| 以不发音的e结尾的动词 | 去e，再+ing | make → making, ride → riding |
| 以"一个元音+一个辅音"结尾的动词 | 双写结尾辅音字母，再+ing | sit → sitting, run → running |

## 五、人称代词 (Personal Pronouns)

### 1. 主格与宾格对照表

| 人称 | 主格代词 (Subject Pronoun) | 宾格代词 (Object Pronoun) |
|------|---------------------------|---------------------------|
| | 单数 | 复数 | 单数 | 复数 |
| 第一人称 | I | we | me | us |
| 第二人称 | you | you | you | you |
| 第三人称 | he/she/it | they | him/her/it | them |

### 2. 用法说明
- **主格代词**：作主语时使用
  - e.g. **Dad** is making breakfast for the children. **He** is good at cooking.
- **宾格代词**：作宾语时使用
  - e.g. **We** are singing birthday songs to Grandma and giving birthday gifts to **her**.

### 3. 代词选择规则
- **she**: 指代女性（第三人称单数）
- **he**: 指代男性（第三人称单数）
- **it**: 指代事物和动物（在我们无需强调性别的时候）

## 六、名词复数 (Plural Nouns)

### 1. 可数名词与不可数名词

| | 可数名词 | 不可数名词 |
|--|---------|-----------|
| 单数形式 | 使用a/an: buy **a** tomato, have **an** apple | 不使用a/an: drink milk |
| 复数形式 | 加-s: want two cucumbers | 没有复数形式 |
| 表示数量 | 使用数词: need **three** oranges | 使用量词表达: buy **a bottle of** orange juice |
| | 使用量词: buy **a bag of** noodles | don't have **much** yogurt |
| | have **many** eggs | eat **a lot of/lots of** cheese |
| | eat **a lot of/lots of** grapes | There is **a little/little** bread left. |
| 询问数量 | 用how many: How **many** carrots would you like? | 用how much: How **much** salt should I add? |

### 2. 名词复数变化规则

| 名词类型 | 变化规则 | 示例 |
|---------|---------|------|
| 大多数名词 | +s | pepper → peppers |
| 以ch, sh, s, ss, x, z结尾的名词 | +es | peach → peaches, dish → dishes |
| 以o结尾的名词 | +s 或 +es | kilo → kilos, tomato → tomatoes |
| 以"元音字母+y"结尾的名词 | +s | day → days |
| 以"辅音字母+y"结尾的名词 | 去y变i，再+es | strawberry → strawberries |
| 部分以f或fe结尾的名词 | 去f或fe变v，再+es | knife → knives |
| 其他名词（不规则） | 词尾加字母（组合） | child → children |
| | 改变元音字母 | goose → geese |
| | 单复数同形 | sheep → sheep |

## 七、特殊疑问句 (Wh- Questions)

### 1. what 和 who 的用法

**what**: 用于询问事物或想法
- e.g. **What** do you think of the sport of rock climbing?
- e.g. **What** are your hobbies?

**who**: 用于询问人
- e.g. **Who** plays basketball for the school?
- e.g. **Who** is really into sports?

**规则**: 
- 当what/who作主语时，不需要使用助动词do/does
- 当what/who作宾语时，需要在主语前添加助动词do/does

### 2. how, when, where, why 的用法

| 疑问词 | 用途 | 示例 |
|-------|------|------|
| **when** | 询问时间或日期 | When do they feed animals every day? |
| **where** | 询问地点或位置 | Where are the goats? |
| **why** | 询问原因 | Why do pigs roll in the mud? |
| **how** | 询问方式 | How do pigeons find their way home? |

### 3. how + 形容词/副词 的用法

| 短语 | 用途 | 示例 |
|------|------|------|
| **how far** | 询问距离 | How far is the goat yard? |
| **how long** | 询问时长 | How long can elephants live? |
| **how often** | 询问频率 | How often do you walk your dog? |
| **how many** | 询问可数名词数量 | How many goats does Uncle Lin have? |
| **how much** | 询问不可数名词数量/价格 | How much milk does a cow produce in a day? |

## 八、冠词 a/an (Articles)

### 1. 基本规则
- **a**: 用于以辅音音素开头的单词前
- **an**: 用于以元音音素开头的单词前

### 2. 使用冠词的情况
- 可数名词单数前：buy **a** tomato, have **an** apple
- 表示"一个"的概念

### 3. 不使用冠词的情况
- 不可数名词前：drink milk (✗ drink a milk)
- 可数名词复数前表示泛指

## 九、频度副词 (Adverbs of Frequency)

### 1. 频度副词列表（从高到低）
100% **always** → **usually** → **often** → **sometimes** → **seldom** → **rarely** → **never** 0%

### 2. 位置规则
- 通常位于主要动词之前
- 但位于be动词之后

**例句**:
- I **sometimes** go running. (主要动词前)
- He **often** plays tennis. (主要动词前)
- I **am** usually busy with my studies. (be动词后)
- She **is** never into sports. (be动词后)

### 3. 有些频度副词可以放在句首、句中或句末
- **Sometimes** I go running.
- I **sometimes** go running.
- I go running **sometimes**.

## 十、附加疑问句 (Question Tags)

### 1. 陈述句后的附加疑问句
- **肯定陈述句 + 否定附加疑问句**
  - Xi'an is a good choice, **isn't it**?
  - We can see a lot of pandas there, **can't we**?
  - They will go by train, **won't they**?
  - Your sister wants to visit the Island of Music, **doesn't she**?

- **否定陈述句 + 肯定附加疑问句**
  - He isn't going to Hainan this winter, **is he**?
  - You can't make the decision now, **can you**?
  - They won't go by plane, **will they**?
  - The bus doesn't stop here, **does it**?

### 2. 祈使句后的附加疑问句
使用 **shall we** 或 **will you**
- Let's take the next bus, **shall we**?
- Let's not go out tonight, **shall we**?
- Return to your seats, **will you**?
- Don't eat food on the bus, **will you**?

## 十一、be going to 将来时态

### 1. 基本结构

| 句型 | 主语 | be动词 | going to | 动词原形 |
|-----|------|--------|----------|---------|
| 肯定句 | I | am | going to | travel around China |
| | She/He | is | going to | travel around China |
| | You/We/They | are | going to | travel around China |
| 否定句 | I | am not | going to | travel around China |
| | She/He | isn't | going to | travel around China |
| | You/We/They | aren't | going to | travel around China |
| 疑问句 | Am | I | going to | travel around China? |
| | Is | she/he | going to | travel around China? |
| | Are | you/we/they | going to | travel around China? |

### 2. 用法
1. **将来的计划或安排**
   - We **are going to** visit the science museum this afternoon.
2. **意图、打算**
   - I **am going to** exercise more often.
3. **预测**
   - It **is not going to** rain tomorrow.

**注意**: 在非正式口语中，有时用 **gonna** 代替 **going to**
