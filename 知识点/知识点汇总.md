## 二、语法规则 (Grammar)

### 1. 基本句型 (Basic Sentence Patterns)

#### SV 主谓结构
- **公式**: S (subject) + V (verb)
- **示例**:
  - The bus **stops**.
  - It **rains**.

#### SVC 主动补结构
- **公式**: S (subject) + V (linking verb) + C (complement)
- **示例**:
  - My favourite colour **is** green.
  - The dress **looks** nice!
  - I **am** sorry.

#### SVO 主谓宾结构
- **公式**: S (subject) + V (verb) + O (object)
- **示例**:
  - Yiming **opens** the schoolbag.
  - They **wear** red scarves.

---

### 2. 一般现在时 (Present Simple)

**用法**:
- 习惯、常规、预定事件
- 普遍真理和事实

**句式变化**:

| 类型 | I/You/We/They | He/She/It |
|------|---------------|-----------|
| 肯定 | like | likes |
| 否定 | don't like | doesn't like |
| 疑问 | Do...like? | Does...like? |

**特殊疑问句**:
- What time **does** your school **start**?
- How **do** you **go** to school?
- Where **do** they **have** class?

---

### 3. 现在进行时 (Present Continuous)

**用法**:
- 说话时正在进行的动作
- 当前一段时间内进行的活动

**构成**: am/is/are + doing

| 主语 | be动词 | 动词-ing |
|------|--------|----------|
| I | am | playing |
| You/We/They | are | playing |
| He/She/It | is | playing |

**动词-ing变化规则**:
| 规则 | 变化 | 示例 |
|------|------|------|
| 大多数动词 | + ing | eat → eating |
| 以不发音e结尾 | 去e + ing | make → making |
| 一元一辅结尾 | 双写辅音 + ing | run → running |

### 8. 频度副词 (Adverbs of Frequency)

**从高到低**: always → usually → often → sometimes → seldom → rarely → never

**位置**: 频度副词通常位于主要动词之前，be动词除外。

**示例**:
- I **sometimes** go running.
- She is **never** into sports.

### 10. 附加疑问句 (Question Tags)

**规则**:
- 肯定陈述 → 否定附加疑问
- 否定陈述 → 肯定附加疑问

**示例**:
- Xi'an is a good choice, **isn't it**?
- He isn't going, **is he**?
- Let's take the bus, **shall we**?
- Don't eat on the bus, **will you**?

---

### 11. be going to 将来时态

**用法**:
- 将来的计划或安排
- 意图、打算
- 预测

**构成**: am/is/are + going to + 动词原形

**示例**:
- We **are going to** visit the museum.
- It **is not going to** rain tomorrow.
